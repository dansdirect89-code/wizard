On Site With Me

I don’t just consult from the side — I’m on the ground with your team every day, enforcing standards and driving real improvements.

Safety first, results always
Every site I work on starts with strong compliance, clear daily structure, and a culture of closing actions properly.

1. Safety & Compliance

  • SWPs/SOPs followed + enforced
  • RAMS & method statements reviewed before work
  • Toolbox talks & pre-start checks recorded
  • Incidents/near misses investigated and closed
  • Contamination control mindset

2. Daily Site Management

  • Daily plan briefed (what/where/priority)
  • Staffing, cover and handover managed
  • Live supervision — issues handled same day
  • RAMS/permits aligned to actual tasks
  • Actions tracked and closed (not just logged)

3. Production, Logs & Reporting

  • Daily production logged + variances explained
  • Traffic and site flow monitored
  • Clear daily reporting and next steps
  • Audit-ready records maintained

4. Traffic & Segregation

  • Safe vehicle movement + controlled routes
  • Proper segregation and banksman coordination
  • Clear one-way systems where possible

5. Maintenance & Uptime

  • Mechanical maintenance reporting daily
  • Defects logged, escalated and followed up
  • Fuel and critical checks completed

6. Quality & Contamination Control

  • Weightbridge discipline and outbound checks
  • Correct waste segregation & storage
  • Spill prevention and rapid response
  • Evidence captured where required
